Project Overview

This portfolio showcases graphic design projects from 2017 to 2024, specializing in creating impactful visual content such as banners, logos, brochures, and promotional materials. The designs focus on creativity, attention to detail, and brand consistency.

Project Execution:

Using Corel Draw as the main tool, I developed a deep understanding of design principles, typography, and color theory. I worked on branding projects for businesses, event promotions, digital marketing assets, and personal branding visuals.

Analysis and Results:

Each project reflects a blend of creativity and attention to detail, with a strong focus on meeting client objectives and maintaining brand consistency. Key areas include:

  • Branding Projects: Consistent design approach to ensure brand identity across various platforms.
  • Promotional Materials: Effective visual content for marketing campaigns.
  • Creative Strategy: Innovative solutions tailored to client needs and preferences.

Conclusion:

Over seven years of experience, I have refined my skills in creating visuals that are both aesthetically pleasing and strategically effective, contributing to successful client branding and promotional efforts.
